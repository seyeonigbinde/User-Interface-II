ABOUT

Onigbinde Seye was the Head of Shared Services in Prudential Mortgage Bank where he oversaw the Finance, Human Resources, Information Technology, Strategy, Business Transformation and General Administration departments of the Bank. He has over Six (6) years in Banking. Prior to joining Prudential Mortgage Bank, he was an IFRS Research Associate in Deloitte where he specialized in IFRS reporting, IFRS training, loan impairment computation, financial modelling and data analysis. In 2013, he joined the Bank as an Accounting Analyst and rose to become the Head, Financial Control in 2014.

Educational Background

Seye is a graduate of Economics from the prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile-Ife and has Advanced Certificate in Financial and Business Analysis from London School of Business and Finance. He is an Associate of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Nigeria (ICAN). He is an Associate member of Institute of Strategic Management of Nigeria and has Diploma in International Financial Reporting from the Association of Chartered Certified Accountants (ACCA).
